Pirate Defense

  • Originality: The game is original because it allows players to shoot cannon balls out of a cannon to hit targets.
  • Technical Merit: The physics and calculation for the velocity of the cannon ball required using math to find the x and y component of the velocity.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to seperate game files into different jave script files which keeps code more organized I also learned how to implement physics to calculate velocity The file seperation was learned from this youtube series https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BABIg2MfG88&list=PLoN_ejT35AEhY4icjiEJ5t2qdunwmQj1R&index=10
  • Prototype Assets: I made the target and cannon ball in paint but I got the other two assets from the folloqing links Boat/Background: https://www.ssbwiki.com/Pirate_Ship#/media/File:SSBU-Pirate_Ship.png Cannon: https://imgbin.com/png/rdXkb0db/cartoon-artillery-png
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I made this game by myself, but the idea isn't very complicated. The youtube link in Postmortem was the template I followed when making my game. He followed the phaser example and showed how to break it into its own file with it's own scene.
  • High Concept: You're a pirate in training and you're learning how to shoot the cannon on a ship.
  • Theme: Theme I chose for this game was Pirate which is shown by the pirate ship and cannon.
  • Mandated Variety: The targets randomly change positions and decrease in size as the player progresses farther into the game. The cannon ball doesn't go exactly where the player's mouse is to add a bit of challenge.
  • Prototype Goal: This Prototype is testing physics manipulation and a difficulty curve with a target that decreases in size.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want players to just have fun shooting a target over and over again. This could be a stress reliever
  • Gameplay: The input for this game is aiming with the mouse and clicking to shoot. The cursor is slighlty offset to add a bit of challenge. The gameplay should be fast with players firing quickly rather than aiming. Once the player misses 3 times they can click again to restart.
  • Strategies: I think players will just shoot at the targets with no real stratigy. Aiming slightly to the right of the targets gives the most sucess.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are a wannabe pirate who just found a ship that will take you. After teaching you the basics, it is now time to learn to fire the cannon. This is your chance to impress everybody.
  • Target Audience: This game is for anybody who wants to just de-stress and shoot some targets. Players who want a competetive experience may enjoy going for a high score.
  • Play Time: The game should take between a few seconds up to a few minutes to play. The targets get smaller which make it harder to play for too long.
